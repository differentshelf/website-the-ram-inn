About Our Historic Cotswold Pub

John & Mel - Your Cotswold Hosts

John and Melissa Thwaite come from a long line of publicans — Melissa's parents and grandparents were all in the trade, and the Ram is very much a family run business.

Having held the Licence at the Ram over 20 years, they know just about everyone and everything locally, from the best Cotswold walks to local events and attractions.

The Pub's Rich History

The premises were originally a farm which had a Cider Room and Sweet Counter as add-on businesses. It was converted to a proper pub in 1811 and has been evolving ever since, maintaining its traditional Cotswold charm.

Originally a Whitbread pub, it is now part of the Enterprise Inns family. 16 years ago, it was extended to provide further dining facilities while preserving its authentic character and stunning views over the Toadsmoor Valley.

A True Cotswold Experience

The Ram Inn offers everything you'd expect from a traditional Cotswold pub - exposed stone walls, wooden beams, a roaring fire in winter, and a beautiful garden with panoramic views in summer. We're proud to be part of the rich tapestry of Cotswold life and hospitality.

Cotswold Walks from The Ram Inn

The Ram Inn is ideally situated for exploring the beautiful Cotswold countryside, with numerous walking routes accessible directly from our doorstep. We're a perfect starting point or a welcome pit stop for walkers exploring the Toadsmoor Valley, Chalford, and the surrounding Cotswold Area of Outstanding Natural Beauty.

Ask John and Mel for their recommended walking routes - from gentle strolls suitable for all abilities to more challenging hikes across the rolling Cotswold hills. After your walk, return to The Ram Inn for a well-deserved pint of local ale and home-cooked meal.
